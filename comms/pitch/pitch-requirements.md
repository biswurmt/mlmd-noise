# Pitch Submission Requirements

## Deliverables

- 0.5-1 page project description write-up
- GitHub repository
- 2-minute video walkthrough
- 3-5 minute pitch deck (you will present this)

## Pitch Deck Structure

Your pitch deck should follow this framework:

### (1) Start with the Problem
- What are you looking to solve?
- Clearly articulate the pain point or gap you're addressing

### (2) Value Proposition
- How does your solution fix the problem you stated?
- **Keep this high-level** — many presentations get too technical here
- Focus on the benefit, not the mechanism

### (3) Technology
- Get technical — show the inner workings of your solution
- Explain how your approach works under the hood
- Demonstrate technical depth and innovation

### (4) Market Analysis
- Who is your customer? (Scientists? Hospitals? Government? Other?)
- Identify your target market and their needs
- Show you understand the commercial or deployment context

### (5) Future Roadmap
- **Team**: Who is on your current team and what is their expertise?
- **Next Steps**: What are the immediate next milestones?
- **Limitations**: Any current limitations or constraints?
- **Solutions**: How do you plan to tackle them?
- **Vision**: What are your long-term goals?
