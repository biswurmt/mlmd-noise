# AI Writing Tropes to Avoid

## Structural Patterns
- **"It's not X, it's Y"** - Binary framing that oversimplifies
- **"The obvious response..."** - Condescending setup
- **"Here is the uncomfortable truth..."** - Forced gravitas
- **"The result has been instructive"** - Pretentious observation

## Punctuation Overuse
- **Em dashes** (—) - Used for dramatic pauses instead of commas or parentheses
- **Semicolons** - Attempting sophistication where a period works
- **Colons** - Setting up explanations unnecessarily

## Stylistic Crutches
- **Concessive structures** - "Not A but B" patterns
- **Adverbial pileup** - "stubbornly non-scalable"
- **Metaphor mixing** - "beachhead" mixed with "entry point"
- **Overstated transitions** - "The expansion path is clear"

## Diction Issues
- **Buzzy abstractions** - "decision velocity," "clinical grounding"
- **Overqualified nouns** - "living knowledge graph"
- **Verb inflation** - "construct" instead of "build," "validate" instead of "check"
- **Prepositional phrases** - "at the point of decision" (redundant)

## Tone Problems
- **Predictable escalation** - Every paragraph tries to outdo the last
- **Forced concision** - Leaving out articles for punchiness
- **Bullet-point thinking** - Short sentences masquerading as insights
- **Startup clichés** - "We are building that product"

## What to Use Instead
- Direct statements without hedging
- Simple conjunctions (and, but, so)
- Active verbs
- Short paragraphs with one idea each
- Evidence before claims
