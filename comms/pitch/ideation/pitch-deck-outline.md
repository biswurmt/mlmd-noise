# Pitch Deck Outline — "Close the Expert Gap"
*Framing: physician shortage / democratized expertise. GTM anchored on ED throughput as the validated beachhead.*

Target: 3-minute presentation. Max 5 slides.

---

**Slide 1 — The Problem**
- WHO projects a 4.3M physician shortage globally by 2030 — you can't train your way out of this
- Healthcare is consuming public budgets: Ontario spends ~$74B/year on health — 40 cents of every program dollar — driven by an aging population projected to reach 25% seniors by 2031 (Statistics Canada)
- Clinical expertise is not scalable — it lives in human heads and inaccessible PDFs

**Slide 2 — The Solution**
- Every AI tool that's tried to fix this has hallucinated; clinicians don't trust them, and they're right not to
- Diagnotix: the first clinical knowledge engine clinicians can actually trust
- Built from primary sources — ACR, AHA/ACC, NICE guidelines — not scraped from the web
- Gives any healthcare worker specialist-level reasoning at the moment of decision

**Slide 3 — Technology**
- Knowledge graph grounded in clinical guidelines: symptoms → diagnoses → tests → treatments
- Self-healing: LLM speculatively generates new nodes; audit pipeline validates against literature
- Speaks medicine natively: SNOMED CT, ICD-10, LOINC, RxNorm interoperability

**Slide 4 — Market**
- *GTM beachhead: ED triage decision support*
  - EDs run at 100–120% capacity; test-ordering bottleneck is a solved, measurable problem
  - Triage nurses are already authorized to order basic tests — Diagnotix amplifies that authority
  - Validated use case: parallel project at SickKids, positive physician feedback confirmed
- *Expansion tier 1:* hospital systems, urgent care networks, family practice
- *Expansion tier 2:* license as clinical knowledge API to health-AI companies

**Slide 5 — Team + Roadmap**
- Alice Chua + Tyler Biswurm — Toronto-based, ML + systems
- Prototype live; SickKids relationship ongoing
- 6 months: clinical validation study + 3 signed ED pilots
- 12 months: expand to primary care; pursue health-AI licensing deals
- Vision: Diagnotix powers every clinical AI system that needs to be trustworthy
