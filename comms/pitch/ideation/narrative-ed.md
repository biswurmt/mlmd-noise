# Pitch Narrative (100–150 words)

Every day, emergency department nurses collect a patient's vitals, note their chief complaint, and make one critical call: what tests to order. They have the training. They have the data. What they don't have is a decade of specialty experience filtering the noise.

That gap costs lives and money. A missed ECG order means a patient waits an extra two hours for a physician to re-examine and re-decide. Multiply that by a 300-bed ED running at 115% capacity on a Tuesday night, and you have a system in failure.

Diagnotix gives clinical staff real-time, guideline-grounded intelligence at the point of triage. It's not a chatbot. It's a clinical knowledge engine — built from validated guidelines, enriched with medical ontologies, and sharp enough to surface the right test order before the doctor ever walks in.
